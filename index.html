About NovaBridge

NovaBridge Consulting Group specializes in cross-border agribusiness advisory, helping grassroots producers, cooperatives, and agricultural enterprises connect with international processors, retailers, investors, and new market opportunities.

Based in the Netherlands, NovaBridge supports sustainable agricultural supply chains between emerging production regions such as Africa, Suriname, and other developing markets and high-value destinations in Europe and beyond.

Founder & Principal Consultant

Olufunke A. Bereola

Olufunke A. Bereola is an economist and international business specialist with interest in agribusiness development, market access, trade linkages, and sustainable value chain growth. Through NovaBridge, she helps clients build responsible and profitable cross-border partnerships.

Special Request Travel Advisory: NovaBridge is based in the Netherlands and primarily offers remote consulting. However, on-site travel to Africa, South America, Suriname, or other locations can be arranged for selected projects on special request.
